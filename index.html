Services we provide to you

Easier, quicker, more reliable medical services.

Online Doctors

AI General Practitioner would help with preliminary diagnosis. After that, the patient would be assigned to an online doctor consultation or an offline appointment to meet the doctor in person with traffic support.

Medicine Delivery

Getting medicine can be as easy as making several clicks. After the online doctor consultation, the app would support medical delivery based on the prescription. Medicine would be delivered directly to the elder’s home.

AI Assistant

Health articles are tailored to the elder’s disease record. What’s more, the patient can ask any question to the personal healthcare AI assistant, with informative answers and reliable information.
